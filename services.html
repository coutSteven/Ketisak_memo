Growing in Pictures

Life book

Childhood

As a child, I was very stubborn and playful. My favorite toy was remote control car and remote control boat. In my early day in school, I didn't like school very much.

Teenage Life

Most of my teenage life, I lived with my relatives in Phnom Penh. In the span over 6 years, I switched to 3 school because I didn't like the environment that I was in. I was a shy and quiet boy. I did not make many friend in my middle or highschool. My biggest obstacle was and up until today is the fear of public speaking.

Adult Life

Part of growing up is making more observation. I was very fortunate that I ended my highschool with positive environment where I was able to grow quickly. Rodwell was my second home. I supposed that as Asian, it was always difficult to ask your parent for your freedom. They were so worry about us, for good. However I wasn't happy with the limited of freedom I had up until last year of high school. My freedom wasn't granted but I must fight for.

Future Goal

Currently, I'm pretty much focus on my present development in Computer science field. There are many interesting field to explore namely blockchain, crytocurrency, machine learning, data scicence, web app...etc. However, wherever I ended up, I have my intention centered around helping people live better and ultimately working with people for people.
